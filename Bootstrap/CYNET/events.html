EVENTS

Treasure Hunt

In this event the participants have to solve the IT riddle which would lead them to some clue. The participants are supposed to reach the treasure with the help of these clues. The team will get the next clue from the place where they got the last clue. The event has three rounds. This is a team event and one team can have 2 participants.

Mystery Room

Mystery Rooms is a challenging race against the clock where the team of 3 players will participate in the game for two rounds. The game participants need to apply deductive reasoning, aptitude and logic to escape. It is the smartest, not the strongest, who escapes faster.

WEB WEAVER

The goal of a web page is to convey information in a manner useful to the reader, similar to the goal in writing a paper. How you convey the information can increase readers' interest as well as their knowledge. It is a contest that assists your unique, creative, radical and commercial approach to webpage designing. The event provides a forum to talented students to display their prowess in the same.
